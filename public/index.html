How Matter Works

Our Diversity Dashboard is built around three core ideas:

  • Understand your company

    Put an end to manually adding data or fiddling with spreadsheets--or worse, making decisions without having the data you need. Matter displays all your diversity data in one place to help you understand where your biggest opportunities lie. We answer questions around: How have trends changed over time? Which parts of the company are getting more or less diverse?

  • Grow in the right direction

    Matter provides you insights and tips to make smart decisions to keep growing a diverse and inclusive culture. See comparison data, as well as tips for initiatives you could launch on keeping growing in the right direction.

  • Share your progress

    Communicate your progress with your company, the management team and the world. We give you customizable graphs and charts you can easily export or link to for your internal reporting or external showcasing of your company’s diversity.
